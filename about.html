Heyo, I'm Daniel - I'm a recent grad from the University of Virginia, with a B.A. in Computer Science and a focus in Psychology.

I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. Growing up, I had a unique perspective on the world - being American, and being black. I learned everyone has a story to tell, and it's important to be open to change and fresh ideas.

I found a love for psychology and design my sophomore year of college. After personal struggles with anxiety and depression, I became fascinated with the way people think, feel, and behave - and how products could help them live more fulfilling lives.

Just recently, I finished up an 8-month personal research project for how college students can better care for their mental health, as well as the role counseling clinics play in this.

Outside of design, I'm a major basketball (and Lebron) fan, and enjoy exploring the green outdoors. My fun fact is placing 3rd in a national slam poetry competition.

I'm always down to collaborate when I have the time. I'm currently hoping to further my career in the design space - reach out and let's chat!
